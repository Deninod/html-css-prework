My Backround

From Reprographics to Retail to Transportation

I started working in Reprographics & Document Management as an account manager. Managing document solutions like online plan rooms, bidding submittals and online print orders for architects, engineers and construction management companies. Also, I helped in troubleshooting production issues and resolve customer service questions and concerns.

Next I went on to work in retail management, first as Sales Manager an then as an Assistant Store Manger. Managed weekly sales reports for several departments. Helped to identidy sales trends and opportunities in merchandising. Coached staff on customer engagement, customer service and reaching sales goals.

Eventually I left retail to work as a dispatch supervisor for Transportation company. My day begins with setting up the day's work board that allows us to see a snapshot of all bus schedules. Primarily we work with 3 school districts and private schools within those districts. We use at least two software applications that are designed for transportation.

  • Transfinder Pro - manages the scheduling of routes for all buses.
  • CalAmp - GPS tracking system for all transportation vehicles
